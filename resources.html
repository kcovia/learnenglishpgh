Resources for Learning English

On this page, you'll find a list of useful websites for learning English. We recommend checking some of them out!

  • Duolingo - a free website for learning English via Spanish, French, Italian, and Portuguese
  • Culturally Authentic Pictorial Lexicon - a dictionary of English words with associated pictures
  • Google Translate - easily the best translation website on the Internet
  • Repeat After Us - a library of freely available English literature and texts
  • BBC Learning English - a news site filled with activities, quizzes, and other learning materials for English
  • Dictionary.com - a dictionary for the English language
  • English-Test.net - a collection of English tests
  • ESL Cyber Listening Lab - a collection of English conversation audio files
  • Activities for ESL Students - even more English tests and exercises!
    • English Idioms - a directory of English idioms
  • Common Errors in English - a collection of common mistakes in English
